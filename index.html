☁️ Google Sheets & Drive Sync
💡 Data always saved locally first (works offline). When connected, saves also push to your Google Sheet and Drive.
Status: ⚪ Not connected
  1. Open Google Drive → create a new Google Sheet.
  2. In the Sheet, go to Extensions → Apps Script. Paste the contents of google-apps-script.gs.
  3. Create a folder in Google Drive. Copy its Folder ID into DRIVE_FOLDER_ID in the script.
  4. Click Deploy → New deployment → Web app. Set "Execute as: Me" and "Who has access: Anyone".
  5. Copy the Web App URL and paste it above, then click Save & Test.
